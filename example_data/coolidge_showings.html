About The Coolidge

  • Directions: 290 Harvard Street, Brookline, MA
  • Phone: 617-734-2500

The Coolidge Corner Theatre is an independent, nonprofit cinema and cultural institution with six screens, an Education & Community Engagement Center, and the capacity for more than 900 audience members. Since 1933, audiences in the greater Boston area have relied on the Coolidge for the best of contemporary independent film, repertory, and educational programming.
